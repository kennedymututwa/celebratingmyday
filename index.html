Happy Birthday
Kennedy

Hi, I'm Kennedy...

and this is my story.

I was born in Central Province...

right here in Zambia.

My early years were at Mboshya Basic...

before I moved to Lusaka, the capital.

After qualifying for 10th grade...

I joined a private school...

and studied there from 2013 to 2015.

Then I completed my 12th grade...

just like any other dreamer.

All I wanted was to study medicine...

but life had other plans.

Every time I applied, something...

always rose up to block the way.

One day, thieves broke in...

and took my laptop bag -

with my scholarship, my acceptance letter...

my bank card, my NRC...

and even my phone, right there.

I had to start again from nothing.

But I remembered...

that God was my last hope.

I wanted nothing but to worship Him.

And God remembered me.

He gave me a place at the hospital in 2019...

and in 2021 opened the door to India.

I went to study Physiotherapy...

and came home with a BCA degree too -

a bonus I never planned for.

Everything was restored.

I have every reason...

to keep worshipping Him.

In Him I am established.

Yes, I'm a Physio...

but the hunger of those hard years...

still pushes me to keep exploring.

When I started, I faced a storm of criticism...

but today I've rewritten that old story.

That's the short version of me.

Now I welcome you...

into my brand-new year -

filled with peace...

joy, and prosperity.

By the way...

I love comedy and animated cartoons...

deep chats about capacity building...

discovering new places...

and meeting well-cultured friends.

Welcome to my story.
